Who we are

Meet the people behind Apex

Apex is led by Dr Ben Phillips and Dr Emily Duncan, who bring together expertise across land-based ecology, marine conservation, social research and data analysis, with a practical, collaborative way of working.

Dr Ben Phillips

Ben brings expertise in ecology, social research and applied conservation, with particular experience in land management and nature recovery.

His work has included pollinator conservation, road verge research, evidence reviews, spatial analysis and research on how people experience, value and act for nature, often in collaboration with NGOs, public bodies and research partners.

Dr Emily Duncan

Emily brings expertise in marine conservation, international project delivery and applied research, with particular experience in plastic pollution and marine megafauna.

She works with conservation organisations and coastal communities on capacity building, engagement and project delivery, and sits on the expert panel for Defra’s Ocean Community Empowerment and Nature Grants Programme.
