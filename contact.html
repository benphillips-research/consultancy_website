Helpful things to include

You do not need to have everything worked out before getting in touch. If useful, you could include:

  • the organisation, project, or place you are working with;
  • the kind of support you may need;
  • whether there is a deadline, funding call, or decision point coming up;
  • any relevant background documents, links, or project ideas;
  • what a helpful next step would look like.

Not sure what you need yet?

That is fine. We can help you work out the next useful step, whether that is evidence synthesis, data analysis, project design, proposal support, or a fresh scientific perspective.
