Detailed services

A closer look at how we can help.

We can contribute to a defined task or provide broader input across research, evidence, analysis, proposals and delivery.

1 Ecology and conservation

We support ecological research across land and ocean systems, from field surveys and experiments to evidence-based advice for habitat management and conservation planning.

Our experience includes:

  • Ecological studies. Designing and carrying out surveys and experiments to answer ecological questions.
  • Habitat and land management. Particular experience with road verges, public green spaces, farmland and pollinator habitats.
  • Marine conservation. Research relating to marine megafauna, fisheries waste and plastic pollution monitoring.

2 Social research

Understanding people’s attitudes, motivations and behaviours can help shape conservation that is more effective, inclusive and realistic.

We have experience with methods including:

  • Questionnaire design. Including surveys of 18,000 adults across England with Natural England, and public attitudes and behaviours relating to pollinators.
  • Stakeholder interviews and focus groups. Including work to guide a conservation strategy for hazel dormouse.
  • Digital data sources. Including integrating survey data with social media and other sources to understand how people engage with nature.

3 Evidence synthesis

We can review, synthesise and communicate evidence to a rigorous scientific standard, helping organisations make use of the best available knowledge.

Our experience includes evidence work on:

  • Environmental links between forests and oceans for The Woodland Trust.
  • Interactions between marine plastic pollution and turtles and other megafauna.
  • Enhancing road verges for pollinators and for wider ecosystem services.

4 Data and spatial analysis

We help organisations manage, analyse and communicate ecological and social data, turning complex datasets into useful evidence.

Our experience includes:

  • Remotely assessing road verge habitats using Google Earth and Street View.
  • Modelling road pollution across Great Britain.
  • Comparing light pollution levels across UK local authorities and habitats.

5 Programme development

We provide scientific input across grant writing and programme development, from shaping early ideas to developing fundable proposals and supporting delivery.

Our experience includes:

  • Grant writing. Support with clear, compelling proposals, drawing on experience as applicants and reviewers.
  • Project and programme support. Scientific input for design, coordination, monitoring, evaluation, learning and reporting.
  • Capacity building and community engagement. Including work focused on gender, equality and social inclusion in environmental initiatives.

Need something else?

If what you need is not listed here, we would still be happy to talk it through. We can help you work out what kind of evidence, analysis or support would be most useful.
