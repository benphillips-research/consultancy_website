Areas of expertise

Evidence across land, ocean and people

Recent projects

Recent applied outputs produced with conservation, research and delivery partners.

Pollinator conservation

Research on pollinator habitats, crop pollination, climate impacts, and public engagement with pollinator conservation.

Roads and road verges

Research on road verge habitats, road pollution and ecosystem services, with practical recommendations for improving verge management.

Marine plastic pollution

Research on plastic pollution, marine turtles, megafauna interactions, and lost or discarded fishing gear, particularly for Small Island Developing States.

People and nature

Research on how people understand, value and respond to nature, including public engagement, practitioner insight, and conservation behaviour.
