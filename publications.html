Evidence behind the work

Research credibility across ecology, conservation, people, and data.

In collaboration with many colleagues and partner organisations, we have published over 50 academic papers, cited more than 3,000 times, alongside reports and applied outputs for a wide range of audiences.

What this shows

Peer-reviewed work across conservation science, ecological research, social science, marine pollution, and environmental decision-making.

A strong research record that reflects work being used, tested, and built on by the wider scientific community.

Applied

Project outputs

Reports, reviews, protocols, and practical evidence summaries designed to support real conservation decisions.

Selected areas of expertise

Pollinator conservation

Research on pollinator habitats, mass-flowering crops, drought, road verges, and public engagement with pollinator conservation.

Roads and road verges

National analyses of road verge habitat, road pollution, ecosystem services, and practical options for improving verge management.

Marine plastic pollution

Work on plastic pollution, marine turtles, megafauna interactions, abandoned fishing gear, and remote island pollution baselines.

People and nature

Studies of public engagement, attitudes, practitioner knowledge, Google Trends, surveys, and conservation behaviour.

Evidence synthesis

Reviews and evidence summaries to help organisations understand complex topics and make better-informed decisions.

Data and spatial analysis

Work using GIS, remote assessment, large datasets, spatial modelling, and digital data sources.
